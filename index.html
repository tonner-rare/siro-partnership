The Approach

We are creating a pipeline from your VoIP solution to Siro — getting your phone calls flowing automatically, attributed to the right reps, and treated as native Siro recordings.

Phase I
Scoping

A rapid conversation to audit your VoIP setup, confirm API access, and ensure technical feasibility. If it's not possible, you'll know before we ever start the clock.

Phase II
Build & Deploy

We build a lightweight, bulletproof integration service. It detects when a call ends, pulls the audio and metadata, and fires it into Siro's API with perfect rep attribution and CRM linking — deployed directly to your cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP).

Phase III
Handoff

You get clean code, clear documentation, and a working integration — no loose ends.
